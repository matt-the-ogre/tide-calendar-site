How to Find Your Tide Station ID

🇺🇸 For USA Stations (NOAA)

To find a USA tide station ID, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the NOAA Tide Stations Page.
  2. Use the map or the search function to find the tide station closest to your location.
  3. Click on the tide station marker to see the station details.
  4. Look for the station ID in the station details (usually a 7-digit number).

🇨🇦 For Canadian Stations (CHS)

To find a Canadian tide station ID, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Canadian Hydrographic Service Stations Page.
  2. Use the map or search to find the tide station you need.
  3. Click on the station to view details.
  4. Look for the station ID (usually a 5-digit number).

Tip: You can also use the search feature on our homepage to search by place name instead of entering a station ID!
